About Us

Founded in 2016, All Things Sweet stems from Chef Sarekaa B Malik’s passion for crafting delicious and artistic cakes. Sarekaa brings over 15 years of culinary expertise to her role as a pastry chef and specialist in cake creation. Having delivered more than 10,000 cakes, and collaborating with over 50 corporates, our team of professional bakers has been creating millions of beautiful memories for our valuable clients. Located in South Delhi, our cloud kitchen specializes in customizing cakes for all occasions, recognizing the unique sweet preferences of every person, offering bespoke cakes and baked products for corporate events and special social occasions. Our commitment to excellence extends to health-conscious options developed through in-depth study and experimentation. We offer a diverse range of healthy yet delicious cakes and bakery products, including gluten-free, sugar-free, lactose-free, keto, and Diabetic Friendly options, all tailored to individual requirements. Beyond this, we present a wide array of choices for festive gifting, wedding favours, and return gifts. Proudly using the finest ingredients, we prioritize taste and hygiene in every creation. Each cake is baked fresh to order, meticulously customized based on detailed client discussions to ensure complete satisfaction and value for money. Our greatest accomplishment lies in winning our clients’ trust. Every cake is a manifestation of love, understanding that each order is made with deep affection for special occasions. At all Things Sweet, we don’t just bake; we curate moments that linger in the hearts of those celebrating.
